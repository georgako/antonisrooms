Kastro

When you see Kastro for the first time, you shall be fascinated by the unique sight of the village, which appears lordly and aristocratically solitary, between the sea and the sky.

Kastro is built on a naturally fortified location, reinforced by a double wall and it maintains both in its name and in many points of its streets the memories of a multifarious and really interesting history.
